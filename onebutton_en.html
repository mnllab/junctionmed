Automatic Conversation Recording at the Start of Care

When you start a care service, just press the tablet's button once, and all conversations will be automatically recorded and converted into text. Say goodbye to the hassle of manual records and the worry of missing information.

Real-time Data Extraction for Health, Emotions, Medication, and more

CareBOM's AI analyzes the conversation content in real-time, accurately extracting and structuring 11 key pieces of information about the senior's health status, emotional changes, medication status, and meals.

Automatic Report Dispatch to Administrators and Family Members

The analyzed data is automatically generated into a professional report in a pre-set format and is securely sent to institution administrators and family members with a single click.
